## Project Overview
RingWave is a next-generation internet audio calling platform that combines real-time voice communication with AI-powered voice authenticity verification.

The application allows users to make one-to-one and group audio calls over the internet while continuously analyzing voice streams to identify whether the voice is likely genuine, AI-generated, cloned, or manipulated.

The increasing availability of voice cloning technologies has created significant security concerns. Fraudsters can now imitate family members, company executives, customer support representatives, and public figures with high accuracy. Traditional communication applications provide no mechanism to identify such threats.

RingWave addresses this challenge by integrating a Deepfake Voice Detection Engine into the calling infrastructure.

During a call, voice samples are analyzed by machine learning models trained on genuine and synthetic speech datasets. The system generates an authenticity score and alerts users whenever suspicious audio characteristics are detected.

## Problem Statement
Recent advances in Artificial Intelligence have made voice cloning highly accessible.

Common threats include:
- Phone scams using cloned family voices
- Financial fraud through executive impersonation
- Fake customer support calls
- Social engineering attacks
- Identity theft using synthesized speech
